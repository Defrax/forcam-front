Overview

FORCAM is a global technology partner to discrete manufacturing companies providing a unified approach for sustained manufacturing excellence across all operations. Cutting-edge software modules work together to promote a deep understanding of Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE).

FORCAM’s awarded technology FORCAM FORCE(TM) monitors the performance of over 60,000 machines globally and is deployed by best-in-class companies active in Automotive, Aerospace and Defense, Oil and Gas, and Medical Device discrete manufacturing industries. Customers achieve productivity increases far over 20% in less than 12 months.

We Deliver Results In Productivity.”

Background

Developed during the height of the recession German technology vendor FORCAM launched its latest software release and flagship offering—FORCAM FORCETM. This manufacturing intelligence solution was developed in Germany with current manufacturing industry needs in mind. FORCAM FORCETM reliably measures and evaluates real-time performance of industrial equipment and facilities within global business arena, thus enabling companies to instantly identify and eliminate errors and waste. Inevitably, FORCAM helps companies to secure brand image, preserve market share, and increase shareholder value.

Detail

The technology monitors the performance of over 60,000 machines globally and is currently deployed by best-in-class manufacturing companies with results:

  • GKN Aerospace realized >20% reduction in operation time (ROI < 1 Year)
