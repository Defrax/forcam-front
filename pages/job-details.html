Key Account Manager (m/f) Shanghai, China

As Key Account Manager (m/f) you will drive business by leading our clients in the sales process. You will also be responsible for generating revenue, based on our sales strategy.

You will be using different sales channels e.g. direct sales, partners and marketing. With excellent sales skills and competent technical background, you will significantly push the market share of FORCAM Shop Floor Management in China and global accounts.

Your Profile

  • Excellent understanding of business and selling process of global companies
  • Extensive experience in account management and / or consulting with proven results
  • You are straight forward, target oriented and can handle sales responsibility
  • Thorough knowledge of discrete manufacturing and lean management
  • Reliable and competent client leadership, pairing sales results with high client reputation
  • Confidence, perfect communication and presentation skills
  • Your organizational skills are outstanding and you are a strong team player
  • You have full working proficiency in Chinese and English, German is an additional benefit

Our Offer

Together with your help, we want to make FORCAM the best in class solution provider.

You will be part of a young and dynamic team developing software and concepts for Industry 4.0. Sophisticated challenges and responsibilties are waiting for you working for international clients. You will be working in a team with German and Chinese colleagues from our office directly in the heart of Shanghai near the exciting and bustling district LuJiaZhui. Besides China you will be cooperating with our offices in Germany and USA in international project teams for our global clients.
